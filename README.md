# bptimer-auto

`bptimer-auto` is a streamlined companion to [`bpsr-logs`](https://github.com/winjwinj/bpsr-logs), focused entirely on tracking Blue Protocol: Star Resonance world boss hp
syncs and reports to https://bptimer.com/

## Download
- Latest builds: https://github.com/LaurynasBickauskas/bpsr-logs/releases/latest

## Feature Highlights
- Boss-only meter overlay that fades in when a boss appears and out when the fight ends.
- Crowdsourced auto-reporting keeps community boss timers accurate without manual submissions.
- No DPS tracking or damage breakdownsâ€”only the essentials for boss awareness.
- Minimal settings and lightweight controls so you can launch and play without configuration.
